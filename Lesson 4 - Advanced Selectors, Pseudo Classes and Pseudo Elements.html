How Magnets Work

Magnets are some of the most fascinating objects in the world. Here are some of their properties.

Magnetic Elements:

  • Iron (Fe)
  • Nickel (N)
  • Cobalt (Co)
  • Steel: (Alloy of iron and carbon)

Magnetic Fields:

Magnetic Fields have field-lines that indicate the direction and strength of the field. The closer together the field lines are, the stronger the magnet field is at that point.
